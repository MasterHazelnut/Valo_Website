Agents

In Valorant, the Agents are divided into four primary roles, each bringing a unique set of skills and strategies to the game. Duelists are the damage dealers, excelling in taking on fights and securing kills. They are often the first to enter combat and are responsible for creating space for their team. Initiators are designed to disrupt enemy setups and create opportunities for their team. They specialize in breaking through defensive positions and gathering crucial information on enemy whereabouts. Controllers are the strategic backbone of any team, equipped with abilities that can shape the battlefield by blocking vision, cutting off areas, and controlling enemy movements. They excel in managing the flow of the game and setting up advantageous situations for their team. Lastly, Sentinels are the defensive experts, focusing on protecting their team and holding strategic positions.
