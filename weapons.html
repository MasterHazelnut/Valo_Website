Weapons

In Valorant, weapons are divided into sidearms and primary weapons, catering to various playstyles. Sidearms, like the Classic, Shorty, Frenzy, Ghost, and Sheriff, are ideal for close-range encounters. Primary weapons include SMGs (such as Stinger and Spectre), shotguns (Bucky and Judge), rifles (Bulldog, Guardian, Phantom, and Vandal), sniper rifles (Marshal, Outlaw and Operator), and heavy weapons (Ares and Odin). Each weapon has unique attributes, including damage output, accuracy, and recoil, making the choice of weapon crucial for tactical advantage in the game. Understanding and mastering these weapons can significantly enhance gameplay.
